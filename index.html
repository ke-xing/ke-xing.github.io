About Me

My name is Ke Xing, and I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the School of Computer Science & Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University. My research interests focus on Video Generation, World Models, and 3D Vision. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yunchao Wei and Prof. Xiaojie Jin in the Wei Lab at the School of Computer Science & Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University. I am always open to long-term internship opportunities and welcome any chance to connect and exchange ideas. Feel free to get in touch with me!
